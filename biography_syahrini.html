Syahrini

Rini Fatimah Jaelani, also widely known as "Syahrini" (born in Sukabumi, Indonesia; August 1, 1982) is an Indonesian singer, songwriter, dancer, and actress of Indonesian nationality. Syahrini spent her childhood in Sukabumi, and later earned bachelor law degree from Pakuan University in Bogor. In 2008, She collaborated with songwriters, including Dewiq and Yovie Widianto for her first pop solo album, My Lovely, two songs from that album "Bohong" and "My Lovely" were released as singles. In the middle of 2009, Syahrini released the single, "Pusing Setengah Mati".

In late 2009, Syahrini met Anang Hermansyah on a TV show who then asked her to be his duet partner. Together they released the singles "Jangan Memilih Aku" and "Cinta Terakhir". In 2010, she recorded a cover version of the late Alda Risma's song "Aku Tak Biasa", and reestablishing her career as an independent solo female singer, after the collaboration with Anang Hermansyah ended.
