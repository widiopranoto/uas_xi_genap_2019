Bill Gates

Bill Gates was born on Oct. 28, 1955. Gates grew up in Seattle. He attended public elementary school. In 7th grade, his parent sent him to lakeside Private School. His favourite subjects were Math and Reading.

Gates graduated from Lakeside at the age of 13. After that he continued his study at Harvard University. He studied Math and Law. He worked to become a lawyer. In 1973, his freshman (1st) year, he met Steve Ballmer current C.E.O of Microsoft. While at Harvard, Gates developed a version of the programming language BASIC for the first micro computer – the MITS Altair. In his junior (3rd) year, gates left Harvard to devote his energies to Microsoft, a company he had begun in 1975.

Nowadays, he is consistently ranked in the Forbes list of the world’s Wealthiest people and was the wealthiest overall from 1995 to 2009 – excluding 2008. According to the Bloomberg Billionaires list, Gates is the world’s richest person in 2013, a position that he last held on the list in 2007.
